# Games

This is a listing of all games available for GeMatrix at the moment.

## [Tic-tac-toe](games/TicTacToe.md)

The classic pen-and-paper game that you'll always tie...unless you know what you're doing and your opponent doesn't.

Also includes a strange variant that makes the game more complex and less trivial!

**Alias(es):** `Xs and Os`, `Noughts and Crosses`

**Variation(s):** `Dan's Variation`

## [Connect Four](games/ConnectFour.md)

The classic four-in-a-row game that, for some reason, has had the box art memed on.

**Alias(es):** `Four-in-a-row`

**Variation(s):** `5-in-a-row`
