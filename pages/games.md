# Games

This is a listing of all games available for Boardcord at the moment.

## [Tic-tac-toe](games/TicTacToe.md)

The classic pen-and-paper game that you'll always tie...unless you know what you're doing and your opponent doesn't.

Also includes a strange variant that makes the game more complex and less trivial!

**Aliases:** `Xs and Os`, `Noughts and Crosses`

**Variations:** `Dan's Variation`
