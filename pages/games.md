# Games

This is a listing of all games available for Boardcord at the moment.

## Tic-tac-toe

The classic board game that you'll always tie...unless you know what you're doing and your opponent doesn't.

Also includes wacky variants that make the game more complex and less trivial!

**Aliases:** Xs and Os, Noughts and Crosses, Tic tac toe, tictactoe

**Variants:** Dan's Variation
